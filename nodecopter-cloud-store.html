FAQ

Does it scale?

Yes! tusd-nodecopter-edition can be scaled horizontally by launching additional drones, and vertically by flying them on top of each other. For additional scalability you can use the node.js ardrone library - as node.js will automatically take care of all the scaling. If you need more storage for your individual drones, you can hook up a USB stick and mount it on your drone: 

mkdir /mnt/usb
mount -t vfat -o rw,users /dev/sda1 /mnt/usb

Is it safe?

Yes, clouds are a great place to hide your data, as attackers will be unable find your drones. And even if they do find one or more of your drones, you can simply fly them to a different location!

How much does it cost?

We are still doing the math on this, but we are confident that the economies of scale will allow us to drive down storage cost significantly over time!
